Inaugural

A visual exploration of presidential inauguration addresses from 1789 to 2009

The presidential addresses set the tone for the new administration of the republic. This is a visual exploration of the words, concepts and ideas that are discussed, disected by their frequency across fifty-six presidencies. The visualizations seek to discover trends in tone and ideas across time, both positive and negative, and to reveal hidden messages embedded in the narratives.
